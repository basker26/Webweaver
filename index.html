Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal 'Crown of the Palace', is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. it was declared a winner of the New 7 Wonders of the World (2000–2007) initiative.

New York is primarily known for its most sought after city on Earth, New York City.
